My name is Caro Diaz, I'm a Miami native and current student at the University of Miami pursuing my MFA in Interactive Media. Before going back to school I was a Marketing Specialist and freelance designer. I began learning to code as a creative challenge and found that it was a good fit for me. My sweet spot is where technology, design, and marketing intersect.

My goal in pursuing a graduate degree is to combine my previous marketing experience and knowledge with my love for technology and design.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy traveling, drinking coffee, and cooking.
